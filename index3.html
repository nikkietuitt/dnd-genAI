The Call to Adventure

In D&D, you and your friends create characters who go on quests in a fantasy setting.

The Dungeon Master (DM) serves as the game's referee and storyteller.

Rolling Dice

Whether you are swinging a sword or jumping a chasm, the d20 decides the outcome.

  • • Roll 1-9: Likely failure.
  • • Roll 10-19: Likely success.
  • • Natural 20: Critical success!

Imagination First

D&D is about choices. There are no "right" answers—only consequences that make the story better.

Forge Your Identity

A hero is more than just stats. They are a combination of heritage and skill.

Human Fighter

"The versatile veteran."

Fighters are masters of martial combat, skilled with every weapon and armor. As a human, your ambition and adaptability have no limits.

High Elf Wizard

"Scholar of the arcane."

Wizards use long years of study to manipulate reality. Elves, with their centuries-long lifespans, are the ultimate practitioners of magic.

Dwarf Paladin

"The unbreakable oath."

Combining Dwarven resilience with holy conviction, Paladins are the immovable objects of the battlefield, bound by sacred duty.

Tiefling Rogue

"Shadows and silver tongues."

Masters of stealth and precision. Tieflings use their natural cunning and infernal heritage to disappear where others are seen.

Dragonborn Barbarian

"The primal storm."

Dragonborn channel their draconic fury into a terrifying rage. In combat, they are primal forces of nature that few can survive.

Half-Elf Bard

"The weave of words."

Bards weave magic through music and speech. Half-elves excel here, walking between two worlds with grace and charm.
